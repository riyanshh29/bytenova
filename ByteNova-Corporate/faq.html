Company

About ByteNova

ByteNova Technologies is an Indore-based software development company specializing in custom software, web development, mobile apps, AI/ML solutions, cloud services, and DevOps. Founded in 2023, we serve startups, SMEs, and enterprises across India.

Our headquarters is at Crystal IT Park, 5th Floor, Vijay Nagar, Indore, Madhya Pradesh – 452010, India. We have satellite teams in Bengaluru and Mumbai, and serve clients across 8 cities in India.

We work across healthcare, retail & e-commerce, education, finance & banking, logistics, food & hospitality, and technology startups. Our solutions are customized to meet the unique needs of each industry.

We are a team of 12 full-time professionals including software engineers, designers, project managers, and quality assurance experts. We also work with a network of trusted freelancers for specialized requirements.

Process

Development Process

We follow an agile methodology: Discovery (requirements gathering), Design (UI/UX prototypes), Development (sprint-based coding), Testing (QA & UAT), Deployment, and ongoing Support. Clients receive regular updates and demos throughout the process.

Project timelines vary based on complexity. A simple web application typically takes 2-3 months, while complex enterprise systems may take 4-8 months. We provide a detailed timeline during the proposal stage.

We use tools like Slack, Microsoft Teams, or email for daily communication. We conduct weekly status calls, sprint reviews, and provide access to project management tools like Jira or Trello for complete transparency.

Support

Support & Maintenance

Yes. We offer comprehensive post-launch support including bug fixes, feature enhancements, performance optimization, and security updates. Our support plans range from basic (business hours) to premium (24/7) coverage.

Critical bugs are addressed immediately with priority response. Non-critical issues are scheduled in the next maintenance cycle. All bugs are tracked in our issue management system with transparent communication.

Technology

Technology & Security

We use a modern tech stack including Python (Django, Flask), JavaScript (React, Angular, Node.js), Flutter, React Native, Java, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, AWS, Docker, Kubernetes, TensorFlow, and more. We choose the best technology for each project's specific needs.

We follow industry best practices including encryption at rest and in transit, secure authentication (OAuth, JWT), regular security audits, GDPR compliance where applicable, and signed NDAs to protect your intellectual property.
