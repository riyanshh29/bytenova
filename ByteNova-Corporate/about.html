Our Story

From a Vision to a Growing Technology Company

ByteNova Technologies was founded in 2023 by a group of passionate engineers and technology enthusiasts who believed that India's startup ecosystem deserved access to world-class software development at affordable prices.

What started as a small team of 4 developers working from a co-working space in Indore has grown into a 12-person technology company serving clients across 8 cities in India. Our journey has been defined by a relentless focus on quality, client satisfaction, and continuous learning.

Today, ByteNova delivers custom software, web applications, mobile apps, AI solutions, and cloud infrastructure to startups, SMEs, and enterprises — helping them leverage technology for tangible business growth.

Our Journey

Company Milestones

Key moments that have shaped ByteNova Technologies into what it is today.

2023 — Q1

Foundation

ByteNova Technologies was founded in Indore by a team of 4 engineers with a vision to make quality software development accessible to Indian businesses.

2023 — Q3

First Major Client

Onboarded our first enterprise client, delivering a comprehensive hospital management system used by a 100-bed multi-speciality hospital.

2024 — Q1

Team Expansion

Grew to 8 team members, adding expertise in AI/ML, mobile development, and cloud infrastructure to our service portfolio.

2024 — Q3

45+ Projects Delivered

Reached the milestone of 45 completed projects across healthcare, retail, education, finance, and logistics sectors.

2025 — Q1

Office Expansion

Moved to our dedicated office space at Crystal IT Park, Vijay Nagar, Indore, and expanded to a team of 12 professionals.

2025 — Present

Serving 8 Cities

Currently serving clients across Indore, Bengaluru, Mumbai, Pune, Delhi, Hyderabad, Chennai, and Ahmedabad.
