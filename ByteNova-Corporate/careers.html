Process

Our Hiring Process

A transparent, respectful process designed to find the best fit for both sides.

Step 1

Submit Application

Apply through our portal with your resume and a brief note about why you're interested in joining ByteNova.

Step 2

Initial Screen

Our HR team reviews your application and schedules a brief call to understand your background and aspirations.

Step 3

Technical Assessment

A practical, role-relevant assessment that evaluates your problem-solving abilities and technical skills.

Step 4

Team Interview

Meet the team you'll be working with. We discuss real problems, collaboration styles, and cultural fit.

Step 5

Offer & Onboarding

If there's a mutual fit, we extend an offer and welcome you to the ByteNova family with a structured onboarding plan.
